# Team Norms

_Last updated: 2026-05-12 | Status: DONE_

## Async-first communication
Default to written, async communication for anything that doesn't need a real-time decision. Slack is for quick questions and urgent signals, not decisions.

- Decisions happen in Linear, GitHub, or the handbook — not Slack threads
- If a decision was made in a call, it gets written down before EOD
- No pressure to respond outside of your working hours

## Working hours and availability
We're a fully remote team spread across timezones. No one is expected to be online outside their stated working hours.

- Post your working hours in your Slack profile
- Mark yourself OOO in Google Calendar with at least 24h notice for planned absences
- Urgent pages go through PagerDuty — don't DM people when you can't reach them

## Meeting rules (moved from separate doc)
- Every recurring meeting has an agenda posted 24h in advance
- No meeting without a stated goal and a decision or artifact expected
- Recurring meetings get a quarterly review date — if nobody updates it, the meeting is cancelled
- Meeting notes go in Notion within 24h

## Disagreements
Disagree and commit once a decision is made. Raise concerns early, not after. If you feel unheard, escalate to Lukas.
